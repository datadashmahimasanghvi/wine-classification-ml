Key Findings

What the data tells us

Flavanoids are most discriminative

Highest feature importance (0.22). Cultivar 1 has the richest flavanoid profile, nearly 3× higher than Cultivar 3.

Proline separates Cultivar 1

Cultivar 1 has a mean proline of ~1115 mg/L, far above the other two. Excellent single-feature separator.

Cultivar 3 low in most phenolics

Nonflavanoid phenols aside, Cultivar 3 scores lower on phenol-related features — a distinct chemical signature.

Near-perfect classification

All three classes are well-separated in feature space. Random Forest achieves 97–100% accuracy with 5-fold CV.

Standardisation matters

Proline (range: 278–1680) dominates Euclidean distances. Always apply StandardScaler before KNN or SVM.

Color intensity correlates with Hue

r = −0.52. Darker wines tend toward lower hue values. Useful for feature engineering and PCA decomposition.
